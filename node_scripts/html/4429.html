Other Information
Became the first democratically elected President of Punjab Youth Congress on 23 December 2008, following the initiative of Shri Rahul Gandhi ji to bring inner party democracy in Youth Congress.
To know the problems being faced by the Aam Aadmi in the countryside, undertook a 45-day Pad Yatra on 1st November 2010, on the issue of "Jawani te kisani nu Bachaiye, nawan Punjab Banaiya"; Crisscrossing all districts, all 13 Parliamentary constituencies and around 72 assembly segments covered 1500 Kms on foot; Also worked extensively on the issue of female foeticide;
Being a grandson of Late Sardar Beant Singh (former Chief Minister of Punjab), feels proud of following his footsteps and taking forward his legacy.
