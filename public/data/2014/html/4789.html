Other Information
Political Arena: Active Member of the Congress Party since 1962 and from 10 June 1999, has been an active Member of the Nationalist Congress Party; started political career as a Sarpanch of Akluj Grampanchayat; actively associated with Cooperative Institutes: (1) Chairman, Maharshi Shankarrao Mohite Patil Cooperative Sugar Factory, Shankarnagar, Akluj, from 1971 to 1988 & October, 1996 to October, 1999; implemented different types of Schemes for the Members of the Cooperative Sugar Factory like growth of Sugarcane, water irrigation; established Distillery Project in the year 1984 & Acetic Acid Project in 1993; founded Shankarrao Mohite Patil Memorial Hall Compost Scheme Methane Gas Project; (2) Member, (since 1971) and Director (since 2011) of the National Federation of Cooperative Sugar Factories, Delhi; Member, National Heavy Engineering Cooperative Society, Pune (1971 to 1988) and Director since July, 1997; (3) Member, Maharashtra State Cooperative Bank from 1971 to 1988 and Director since November, 1998; (4) as Adviser, Director of Shankarrao Mohite Patil Cooperative Bank (since 1975) financially assisted by disbursing loan to Small agricultural land holders, Farmers & Small Entrepreneurs; (5) Mentor and Director, Shri Shankarrao Cooperative Sugar Factory, Sadashivnagar, since 1979. Established Distillery Project at Sadashivnagar (Crushing capacity has increased upto 2500 metric tonnes a day); (6) as Director of Solapur District Central Cooperative Bank (since 1979), opened various branches in Solapur District; (7) Managing Trustee, Vasantdada Sugar Institute, Manjari, Pune, since June, 1989; Member and Director, Indian Farmers Fertilizers Cooperative Society, Delhi; President, All India Ethanol Association; (8) Acted as Board of Director, State level Confederation, Maharashtra State Sugar Factories for 14 years. Chairman, Confederation of Maharashtra State Sugar Factories December, 1995 to December, 1997 and also from 17 December, 2010.
Actively associated with various constructive activities; achievements include: Plantation; constructed 56 village ponds; built 710 Public wells for peoples to overcome shortage of water; carried out repairs and maintenance of old wells, wisely managed the distribution of water for agriculture through canal under which 30,000 hectares land came under the irrigation, ultimately production of grains increased; In 1972, during the drought situation in the Solapur district, supplied free water tanker to peoples of 11 Talukas in Solapur district and Indapur Taluka of Pune District; also provided fodder for cattle free of cost; Cattle sheds were also opened for cattle; Implemented One Village – One water tank; additional Lands were distributed among the Backward Class and landless people; founded institute which helped the farmers to undertake Milk business as an additional source of income; More than 90% of Electrification work has been done in Malshiras Taluka
