Social And Cultural Activities
He has passionately involved himself in reaching out to poor and destitute of the district in particular and the State as a whole by way of distributing relief materials, blankets etc., during floods and cyclones and severe wintry conditions.
He is a Trustee of RKJ Foundation, a Charitable Trust which conducts wide spread philanthropic activities like Medical camps in rural areas for free treatment of disadvantaged citizens, setting up Relief Camps for Flood & Cyclone affected poor people, helping poor parents for marriage of their daughters, arranging trainings for poor farmers for better agrarian outputs, educational scholarships, plantations, promotion and encouragement of Oriya artists

Other Information
He was instrumental in turning around the loss making; BIFR-referred Ferro Alloys Company to highly profitable and leading Ferro Alloys Company within a span of two years. He has had several exposures overseas, including heading a Ferro Alloy Plant in Europe. His immense contribution to the Ferro Alloys Company and continuous commitment and support to the society & it’s various causes and for the protection of the environment, has earned him numerous accolades and Awards which include : Rajiv Gandhi Sadbhavana Award – by Rajiv Gandhi Forum, Odisha; Utkal Samman – 2004; Arch of Excellence (Business) Award – 2004; Gem of India Award – 2004; Maharaja Sri Ramchandra Bhanjdeo Award; Industrial Promotion in the State Award – 2005 by Governor of Odisha; Rajiv Gandhi Rastriya Ekta Samman – 2005, New Delhi; State Level Excellence Award – 2005 By Hon’ble Chief Minister of Odisha; Bhartiya Vikas Ratan Award – 2007; Bharat Gourav Award by Institute of Economic Studies – 2008; Award of Excellence by Hon’ble Governor of Odisha for Highest Relief Distribution during flood in Balasore District – 2008; Pride of the Nation Award – 2008; Think Odisha Leadership Award – 2010 for Best Education Supporty by Hon’ble Governor of Odisha; Best Citizens of India Award – 2010
